Meet RASHMI

A compassionate and experienced psychologist with over 25+ years of dedicated service in helping individuals heal and grow. With specialization in child psychology, relationship counseling, depression, and drug addiction, She has guided countless people towards a path of recovery and wellness. Her dream was to make support accessible to all, leading her to establish this NGO. She offers free counseling sessions and spreads awareness about mental health and addiction. With a warm and empathetic approach, she welcomes you to reach out for support and guidance.

We Support

SWF provides supportive counseling services for individuals struggling with a range of mental health issues, including:

Depression and anxiety

Trauma and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)

Drug abuse and addiction

Eating disorders and body image issues

Relationship challenges and family conflicts

Grief and loss

Stress and adjustment disorders

Personality disorders and emotional regulation difficulties

Mental health issues related to chronic illness or disability

Our compassionate and experienced counselors offer a safe and non-judgmental space to explore your concerns, develop coping strategies, and work towards healing and recovery. We believe in empowering individuals to take control of their mental health and wellbeing, and we're here to support you every step of the way.
